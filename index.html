About Me: Katherine Aristizabal

Introduction

Hi! My name is Katherine Aristizabal and I am a student at Northeastern studying Computer Science with a minor in Psychology and a concentration in human centered computing. Throughout my time at Northeastern University I have done 3 co-ops as a software engineer at Johnson & Johnson doing backend engineering, and at Notability and Apple doing iOS development. If you want to learn more about my experiences you can check them out in my my CV page. I am really interested in continuing to do iOS development full time and am always open to talking to new people about it!

I was born and raised in Lowell MA and am also Colombian. On campus I served as the vice president for our Latin American Student organization as well as a program manager for our PODER first gen latinx mentorship program. In my free time I love to read, travel and try new foods. My top three favorite cuisines are Thai food, Korean food and Mexican food.
